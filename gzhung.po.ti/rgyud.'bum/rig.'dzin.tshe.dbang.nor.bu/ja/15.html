Bibliographic and Informative Colophon F. 172b brgya don gnyis L1 to F. 172b brgya don gnyis L5
//rgya gar gyi mkhan po bi ma' la mi tra dang/ /bod kyi lo tstsha ba dran pa ye shes kyis bsgyur cing gtan la phab nas/ /myang ting 'dzin bzang pos lcags sgrom nag po'i nang du/ /bram ze'i rgyud drug gdams pa dang bcas pa// bse sgrom smug por bcug nas/ /dbu ru bzong phug mo'i brag la mkha' 'gro ma rdo rje rgyan cig ma dang// badzra sa dhu legs pa rtsal la btad nas gter du sbas pa rdzogso// // rdzogs pa chen po lta ba'i yang snying// sangs rgyas thams cad kyi dgongs pa/ /namkha' klongs yangs kyi rgyud ces bya ba/ /gser gyi dril shing can 'di/ /dang po ngo bo nyid kyi skus/ /rgyal ba kun tu bzang po la bshad/ /des rdo rje sems dpa' la bshad/ /des dga' rab rdo rje la bshad/ /des bram ze bde mchog snying po la bshad// des shri sring ha la bshad// des bi ma' mi tra la bshad/ /des tinge 'dzin bzang po la bshad// der gter du sbas/ /'brom. yees bla ma'i bu/ / 'brom ye shes snying pos gton nas// des myang shes rab mchog gi sras/ /des shangs kyi ston pa lce chung pa ye shes rgyal mtshan la bshad// des bla ma gnyen chung ba la nang ba'o// //
[Presented as gTer ma]
[ Note that gTing skyes (201.7-202.5) shares this colophon, but in gTing skyes, the order is reversed. The first part of the colophon given here, from “//rgya gar gyi mkhan po bi ma' la” to “ba dzra sa dhu legs pa rtsal la btad nas gter du sbas pa rdzogs so//”, is given after the second part beginning with, “rdzogs pa chen po lta ba'i yang snying// sangs rgyas thams cad kyi dgongs pa/”, continuing up to, “des bla ma gnyen chung ba la nang ba'o//”. mTshams brag (306.4-307.3) also shares the order given in gTing skyes. ]
