[ Note that there are variations in the given titles. Kaneko has: "thig le'i ngang las shar ba yi// thabs dang shes rab kyi rgyud// srog gi thigs pa/ bdud rtsi'i bum pa/ snang byed kyi spu gri'i rgyud/" and "snang byed spu gri 'bar ba'i rgyud rdzogs mtha' can". Title in Virginia catalogue is given as: "spyod pa thabs dang shes rab kyi rgyud/ /srog gi thigs pa bdud rtsi'i bum pa/ /snang byed kyi spu gri'i rgyud/ /yang ti nag po'i rgyud/ /rim pa nas snyan du brgyud pa/ spyod pa'i rgyud/". However, we have compared the texts and they are equivalents. ]
