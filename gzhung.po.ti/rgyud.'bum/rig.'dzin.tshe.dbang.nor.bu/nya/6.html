Nya: Text 6 - Rig 'dzin Tshe dbang nor bu Nya 6, in 4 chapters

[Doxographical category - rDzogs chen - Man ngag sde/Yang ti'i skor. ]

Locations in various editions:
Kaneko 135 ; Tb.107 ; perhaps sDe dge vol. Kha.7 folios 186v-187v, or Ra.2 17r-18r, or Ra.3 18r-19r ; gTing skyes Vol. Nya: 121-123 ; mTshams brag vol Nga pages 574.1-577.2 ; Taipei no 4566 vol 54 pages 471-472.

[ Note that the titles, homage, and chapter titles for this text are similar or identical to those of Nya 4. However, the content of these two texts is clearly different in all editions. The two texts appear to be equated in the Virginia catalogue; they give the wrong Taipei no. for Nya 6, simply listing it as the earlier text. ]
