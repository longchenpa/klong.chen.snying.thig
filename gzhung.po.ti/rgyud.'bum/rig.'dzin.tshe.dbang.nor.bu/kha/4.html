Codicological note [Missing or misplaced folia and other numbering anomolies]


[ We have ch. 5 and ch. 4 references in the wrong numerical order. However, in a comparison with gTing skyes, what appears to have happened is that our folios 63 and 64 are in the wrong order: 64 should be marked as 63, and 63 should be marked as 64. Thus, our ch. 4 runs 62a line 2 up to 62b line 7, then on to 64a line 1 - 64b line 1. Chapter 5 begins 64b line 1, runs on to 64b line 7, then to 63a line 1, up to 63a line 4. Chapter 6 begins 63a line 4, continues through the remainder of 63, and then to 65a. ]
