Ma: Text 3 - Rig 'dzin Tshe dbang nor bu Ma 3, in 1 unnumbered chapter

[Doxographical category - Maha'yoga - sGyu 'phrul gyi skor. ]

Locations in various editions:
Kaneko 204 ; Tb.442 ; gTing skyes Vol. Ma: 142-147 ; mTshams brag vol Za pages 692.7-699.2 ; Taipei no 4897 vol 58 pages 202-203..

[ There are some differences from the mTshams brag edition: the Sanskrit title in mTshams brag is preceded by guhyagarbha a ba ra ta and followed by dva li su ka na tantra (page 692); the following Tibetan title in mTshams brag is preceded by gsang ba snying po de kho na nyid nges pa'i .. and adds at the end, sgyu 'phrul dra ba'i rgyud. However, the homage is the same in mTshams brag (p. 693), and the opening formula is also similar in mTshams brag (page 693); the colophon is probably similar, if one makes allowance for significant recensional variation (page 699). ]
