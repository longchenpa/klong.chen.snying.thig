[ mTshams brag misnumbers chapters from ch. 11, which it gives as a second ch. 10, and all subsequent numbers then run one behind this edition. Our final chapter, 54, however, has an amended numbering, to make it (our second) ch. 53, which would be consistent with the final numbering of mTshams brag and gTing skyes, but is presumably, in fact, a scribal error. Kaneko lists 53 numbered chapters for gTing skyes. Ch. 1-9 correspond, but we have an extra title inserted as our ch. 10 (also given as the first ch. 10 in Virginia). Our ch. 11 corresponds to Kaneko’s ch. 10 (and Virginia’s second ch.10), and all the subsequent numbers run one behind this edition. ]
