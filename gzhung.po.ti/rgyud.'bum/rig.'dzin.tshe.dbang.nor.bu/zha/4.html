zha.4

Zha: Text 4 - Rig ’dzin Tshe dbang nor bu Zha 4 , in chapters 1-7 (le’u 1-3, rtogs pa 4-7), followed by le’u 22 (sic), then phyi ma, with 5 gtogs pa followed by a le’u 7, no evidence of any 6, then gsang ba'i rtog par gtogs pa 1-4, then gtogs pa 8, rtogs pa 9, and gtogs pa 10

[Doxographical category - Maha'yoga - 'Jam dpal gshin rje'i gshed po'i rgyud skor. ]

Locations in various editions:
Kaneko 230 ; Tb.515 & Tb.516 & Tb.517 ; gTing skyes Vol. Zha: 38-100 ; mTshams brag vol Ha pages 2-78.1, 78.1-102.5, 102.5-156.5 ; Taipei no 4970 vol 59 pages 454-46 , Taipei no 4971 vol 59 pages 465-468, Taipei no 4972 vol 59 pages 468-476 ; sDe dge Volume Ma (7th text)..

[ The discrepancies in chapter numberings in this text - or texts - are shared by mTshams brag: the text in our edition closely parallels that in mTshams brag. In Taipei, and in the Virginia Catalogue, the chapters are broken up into three separate consecutive texts. Here, we have provisionally kept in the same text the chapters following what appears to be the root text, since the title at the beginning appears to group them together, and there are no clear separate text titles heading our subsequent sections, apart from the title to the phyi ma immediately following the first group of chapters or root text (although this is absent in mTshams brag). We have divided the text into the group of the first, apparently root text, chapters, and the group of subsequent chapters. The text in the gTing skyes edition only consists of the first chapters (1-7, 22). ]
