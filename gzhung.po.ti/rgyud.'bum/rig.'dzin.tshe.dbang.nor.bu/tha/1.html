Tha: Text 1 - Rig 'dzin Tshe dbang nor bu Tha 1, in 6 chapters

[Doxographical category - rDzogs chen - Man ngag sde/Yang gsang bla na med pa'i skor rgyud bcu bdun. ]

[Possibly presented as gTer ma]

Locations in various editions:
Kaneko 155 ; Tb.290 ; sDe dge vol. Ga.11 folios 324r-379 ; gTing skyes Vol. Tha: 386-530 ; mTshams brag vol Na pages 2-173.3 ; Taipei no 4746 vol 56 pages 118-142.

[ The exact relationship between this version and Kaneko 155 is yet to be determined. Chapters 1-3 and 5 have parallel titles, but ch. 4 has a different title, and while Kaneko 155 does not appear to have a ch. 6, this edition does. Our version seems to correspond more closely to the mTshams brags edition, Virginia's catalogue Tb.290 (Volume Na). ]
