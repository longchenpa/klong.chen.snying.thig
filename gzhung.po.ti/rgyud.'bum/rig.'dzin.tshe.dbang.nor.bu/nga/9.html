[Doxographical category - rDzogs chen - Klong sde. ]

Locations in various editions:
Kaneko 64 ; Tb.45 ; sDe dge vol. Ca.16 folios 195r-203v ; Nubri Nga4.16? with 10 chapters ; gTing skyes Vol. Nga: 152-174 ; this text shares much with mTshams brag vol Ka pages 837.3-863, Taipei no 4504 vol 54 pages 120-124, BUT Taipei 4504 has only 7 chapters, and it seems to conflate chapters that are separated here ; see notes below at chapter endings..
