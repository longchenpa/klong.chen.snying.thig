Bibliographic and Informative Colophon F. 192a brgya go gnyis L6 to F. 192b brgya go gnyis L2
//dngos grub mchog rnyed ya mtshan chen po'i bka'// thams chen bla na med pas gsang sngags 'bras bu'i chos/ /kun gyi thun mongs ma yin bla med man ngag rgyud/ /tshig don rim pa sla yang don nyid rtogs par dka'// bka' rgyud dam pa'i man ngag bla med 'di lta bu// bdag 'dra ba'i ma' la'i man ngag dam pa 'di yin te// dri med zla shar yi dam chosu gtad kyis thub par chos// dri med zla shar yi dam man ngag dam pa 'di// yang dbang gter la gtad kyis bdud rtsi ste// legs pa'i sgron me yi dam nar mar btad// sa ma ya kha tham/ rgyudum bu gsum pa/ lung bam po brgyad/ man ngag dngos grub nye ba'i snying po'i rgyud gsum ni/ sru legs pa'i sgron ma la gtado rdzogs-ho//

[ Note that mTshams brag shares this colophon; the Virginia catalogue suggests that it includes information on their series Tb.616-23, and Tb.624-6. In the case of our edition, the equivalent texts to Tb.616-25 also occur above in this volume, but not immediately prior to this one. Tb.616-23 corresponds to our La 2 (thams cad bdud rtsi lnga'i rang bzhin nyer ba'i snying po bdud rtsi mchog gi lung bam po brgyad pa) and Tb.624/5 corresponds to our La 3 (thams cad bdud brtsi'i rang bzhin gyi rgyud), given in one dum bu (dum bu gsum pa). The colophon appears to say that these texts were transmitted along with this text (La 9). ]
