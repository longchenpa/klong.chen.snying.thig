Za: Text 22 - Rig ’dzin Tshe dbang nor bu Za 22, in 76 chapters

[Doxographical category - Maha'yoga - 'Jam dpal gshin rje'i gshed po'i rgyud skor. ]

Locations in various editions:
seems to be another version of (or a text closely related to) Rig ’dzin tshe dbang nor bu Wa 1 (=Kaneko 226 ; Tb.468 ; mTshams brag vol Ra pages 2-283.4 ; Taipei no 4923 vol 58 pages 481-521) ; Kaneko 264 ; gTing skyes Vol. Za: 414-639 ; although mTshams brag has several “zla gsang nag po” texts, this particular version has not yet been located in mTshams brag..

[ Allowing for scribal errors and minor variations, the chapter titles in this text agree with those of Rig ’dzin tshe dbang nor bu Wa 1 up to and including ch. 71, but the last few chapters differ. However, the relationships between the texts are not straightforward. There may be substantial correspondences even in the final chapters with differing titles (eg. there are at the very least shared passages in ch. 72 and 73), yet at the same time, there may be considerable differences in content in some of the chapters with similar or identical titles. In a comparison of Za 22 against the text in mTshams brag Ra (2-283), we find that while the texts begin virtually the same in the first three chapters, some later chapters, such as the end of ch. 62 and beginning of ch. 63 (mTshams brag Ra: 225) are different in content despite the similar title. In ch. 55, there are some signs that the two chapters might be extremely corrupted descendants of the same original, but it is more likely that this is not the case. However, ch. 10 seems to be the same in both editions, and the mantras given at the beginning and end of ch. 40, are essentially the same. Ch. 20 is substantially similar, but both editions have many scribal errors and variants. A great deal of editorial work would be necessary to clarify these relationships further. ]
