Ha: Text 1 - Rig ’dzin Tshe dbang nor bu Ha 1, in 52 chapters

[Doxographical category - Maha'yoga - Phur pa 'phrin las skor. ]

Locations in various editions:
Kaneko 350 ; Tb.658 ; gTing skyes Vol. Ha: 2-188 ; mTshams brag vol Chi page 2-229.5 ; Taipei no. 5101 vol. 61 pages 132-164.

[ Note that there is some muddle over chapter numbering, both in Rig ’dzin Tshe dbang nor bu and mTshams brag, but in different ways in each. Our edition gives two ch. 4s and omits ch. 6; mTshams brag lists two ch. 1s and omits ch. 21. However, the chapter titles in our edition, gTing skyes and mTshams brag correspond with each other, except that mTshams brag lists 55 chapters. Our edition misses about a folio of text which is found in the mTshams brag edition. Our chapters 51 and 52 titles correspond with mTshams brag’s 54 and 55 respectively, while mTshams brag’s chapter titles for its chapters 51-53 are missing in our edition (and the Kaneko catalogue). A comparison between our edition and mTshams brag shows that after ch. 50, ch. 51 begins with the same opening phrase in both editions. Our text then jumps to mTshams brag’s ch. 54 (thus missing text from mTshams brag 226.3-228.1), taking up the text after the first opening phrase, which is different. ]
