'a.5

'A: Text 5 - Rig ’dzin Tshe dbang nor bu ’A 5, in 21 chapters, divided into sections termed, “skabs”

[Doxographical category - Maha'yoga - Padma dbang chen rta mgrin skor. ]

Locations in various editions:
Kaneko 276 ; Tb.568 ; gTing skyes Vol. Ya: 329-362 ; mTshams brag vol. Ki pages 431.4-475.4 ; Taipei no. 5021 vol. 60 pages 167-173.

[ The chapters in this text are divided into subject divisions called, "skabs". The first two chapters introduce the subject matter of the text as a whole, and although the beginnings of each skabs are not explicitly marked, it would seem likely that the first begins with chapter 3, while the subsequent skabs each appear to begin after the end of the previous division. However, this is not entirely certain; Virginia’s catalogue of mTshams brag takes the first skabs as beginning at the beginning of the first chapter, and this may also be the case in this edition. ]
