[ Note: This appears to suggest that ch 1 was missing in the original copy, and should be copied from another source if one can be found. gTing skyes (608) also omits chapter 1, but has no such corresponding note: its text continues with the text as we have it, without the note. A comparison with the edition in mTshams brag, which does have a complete chapter 1 (2.1-6.7), establishes that we are missing the whole of ch. 1 apart from the opening context, and about the first two and a half lines (ten and a half yig rkang) which open ch. 2 (6.7-7.3). ]
