Tsha: Text 4 - Rig 'dzin Tshe dbang nor bu Tsha 4, in 8 rim par phye ba, the first with 5 tshul

[Doxographical category - Maha'yoga - Tantra sde bco brgyad. ]

Locations in various editions:
Kaneko 219 ; Tb.629 ; gTing skyes Vol. Tsha: 449-566 ; mTshams brag vol Ngi pages 206.6-359.3 ; Taipei no 5074 vol 60 pages 492-514.

[ We take the first chapter (rim par phye ba) to include within its body 5 sections (tshul), running from the beginning of the text to the reference for the first rim par phye ba on 228b.6. However, Virginia take the 5 tshul to be separate chapters, and the first rim par phye ba to be a sixth chapter following the tshuls. We based our decision partly on the fact that the content of the five tshul (the man.d.alas of qualities, body, speech, mind and activities) appear to be subsumed under the title for this rim par phye ba (all the man.d.alas). It will be necessary to examine the content of the text very carefully to ascertain which of the alternatives is more sound. ]
