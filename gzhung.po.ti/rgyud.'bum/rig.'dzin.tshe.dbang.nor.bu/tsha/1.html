Tsha: Text 1 - Rig 'dzin Tshe dbang nor bu Tsha 1, in 17 chapters

[Doxographical category - Maha'yoga - Tantra sde bco brgyad. ]

Locations in various editions:
Kaneko 216 ; Peking Kanjur 463? ; gTing skyes Vol. Tsha: 2-184. Not so far found in mTshams brag or Taipei, but we do find close correspondences with Kaneko 218 (see note below).

[ Rig 'dzin Tshe dbang nor bu Tsha 3 /Kaneko 218 has parallels with this text: the chapter titles of Chapters 1-7 seem to correspond; Chapters 8-14 found here are not in our Tsha 3 /Kaneko 218, but the Chapter 15-17 titles do correspond to Tsha 3 /Kaneko 218, Chapters 8-10. ].
