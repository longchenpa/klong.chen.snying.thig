Wa: Text 1 - Rig ’dzin tshe dbang nor bu Wa 1, in 74 chapters

[Doxographical category - Maha'yoga - 'Jam dpal gshin rje'i gshed po'i rgyud skor. ]

Locations in various editions:
Kaneko 226 ; Tb.468 ; gTing skyes Vol. Wa: 2-237 ; mTshams brag vol Ra pages 2-283.4 ; Taipei 4923 vol 58 pages 481-521.

[ Barber gives as possible Kaneko 226 equivalent the following: mTshams brag vol La pages 2-259.7; Taipei no 4926 vol 59 pages 1-37. But the texts seem quite different; however the text in mTshams brag is a mess, starting with ch 3. But elsewhere Barber suggests Taipei 4923 with a query as a possible equivalent, and this seems to be correct. mTshams brag numbers Ch. 53 as Ch. 54, and from that point its chapter numbering is one digit ahead of ours and gTing skyes’. Note that there is another version of this text in our Volume Za: Rig ’dzin Tshe dbang nor bu Za 22 , in 76 chapters, starting 187b. ]
