Pha: Text 6 - Rig 'dzin Tshe dbang nor bu Pha 6, in 33 chapters

[Doxographical category - Maha'yoga - sGyu 'phrul gyi skor. ]

Locations in various editions:
Kaneko 191 ; Tb.420 ; gTing skyes Vol. Pha: 415-549 ; mTshams brag vol Wa pages 417.2-580.5 ; Taipei no 4875 vol 57 pages 586-609.

[ An interesting feature of this text is that both in this edition, and in Kaneko’s catalogue, we find a reference announcing the start of a first “bam po”, given after the Tibetan title. No further “bam po” appear to be marked, but the Virginia catalogue notes that the mTshams brag edition has full references to five “bam po” (1: ch. 1-15; 2: ch. 16-28; 3: first part of ch. 29; 4: within ch. 29; 5: final part of ch. 29 - ch. 33). It would seem likely that these references were at some stage removed from an ancestor of our and gTing skyes’s editions. Interestingly, we have a similar example in Volume Dza (Rig 'dzin Tshe dbang nor bu Dza 1 ), where we find a reference announcing the start of a first “bam po”, given after the Tibetan title. No further “bam po” appear to be marked. In the case of that text, all the editions lack the further bam po references. ]
