1) yang le shod (ཡང་ལེ་ཤོད་) Pharping, near Kathmandu, Nepal; site where Padmasambhava subdued non-human beings and practiced Vajrakīla.
2) ma ra ti ka (མ་ར་ཏི་ཀ་) 'chi ba mthar byed (འཆི་བ་མཐར་བྱེད་) Khotang District, Nepal; where Padmasambhava and Mandāravā attained longevity vidyādhara level by practicing with Amitāyus; known as Halesi locally.
